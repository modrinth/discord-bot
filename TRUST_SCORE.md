# Trust Score

Unique Trust Score-based Auto Moderation system tailored for Modrinth discord server.

By default, every person will have trust score of 50, 0 is the lowest (red) and 100 is maximum (green).

It's important to keep in mind that community members cannot affect other's trust score - only auto mod and moderators are able to change it.

## Trust Penalties

Punishments for each violation, each of those are handled by auto moderator.

| Violation                        | Penalty |
|----------------------------------|---------|
| Nickname Policy                  | 1       |
| Spam                             | 5       |
| Unknown links                    | 10      |
| Account age is less than a month | 10      |
| Default profile picture          | 5       |
| Weird username                   | 10      |

## Trust Gains

| Source              | Gain |
|---------------------|------|
| Active Role Granted | 20   |
